Founded in 2023 in a small apartment basement in Lviv, Ukraine, The Basement Boys started as a group of close friends with a shared passion for Counter-Strike. What began as late-night games and improvised tactics soon evolved into a disciplined esports team with a relentless drive to improve. Known for their creative strategies, precise grenade lineups, and unbeatable team synergy, the Boys quickly made a name for themselves in the CS2 community.
